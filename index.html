Things Bree Can't Eat an ever growing list

We All Love Bree

It's a fact!

But one thing we don't love is having to remember her long list of allergies when we are cooking or ordering food for her...that is why this website exists!

Without further adieu, here is what she can't have:

Gluten

Bree can't have anything that was made with wheat or has come in contact with wheat flour.

Dairy

Butter, cheese, milk, cream and yogurt. Some things that are "lactose free" still have "casein" which also bothers her tummy, so check those packages!

There are readily available substitues for each of these such as Earth Balance, and coconut, soy, almond based milks/cheeses/yogurts.
